How It Works

Welcome to our Waste Food Management Application, a powerful tool designed to combat food waste and promote community-driven sustainability. Here's a quick guide on how the application works:

User Registration

Start by creating an account to access the full functionality of the platform. Choose between the "Donor" and "Recipient" profiles based on your role.

Donor Input

Donors can log in and provide details about surplus food, including type, quantity, and expiration date. The intuitive interface ensures a hassle-free input process.

Real-Time Listings

Once submitted, the surplus food information becomes part of the real-time listings. Recipients can browse available donations based on location, type, and quantity.

Claim and Connect

Recipients can claim suitable donations and contact donors through the platform. A secure messaging system facilitates communication between donors and recipients.
